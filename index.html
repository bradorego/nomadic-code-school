What You Will Learn

On day one, you will start with the basics by literally creating your first webpage via a text editor. You will learn front end development, building a variety of browser-based applications, and you will dig into the back end and build server side apps using NodeJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, and host them on Amazon Web Services.

By the end of the program, you will have the skills to be a full-stack, MEAN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, AWS, and NodeJS) developer and even a couple completed projects for you to showcase in your portfolio. You will be ready to get a job, collaborate with others, and start your own projects.

Pricing

Sounds amazing. So how much does it cost?

Right now, you pay $15,000 for the entire program. That includes:

  • Half-week orientation to shake off jet lag, relax, meet your cohort, and get ready for class.
  • 3 months of class, office hours, and career advice with our expert staff.
  • 3 months of housing and travel to exotic places.
  • 6 months of mentorship from our awesome team of mentors.
  • (At least) Weekly community-building events.
  • "Coder Fuel" - aka coffee, snacks, anything else you'll need to power through.
  • Connections to our partners and sponsors.
  • Demo Day to show off your newfound skills and network with potential employers.

We'll let you compare the total cost and what's included with some of the other (conventional) code schools out there.
